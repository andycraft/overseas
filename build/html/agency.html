Description

Situated on the sort after Honey Lane in Waltham Abbey is this four bedroom 1930's semi detached house. The property benefits from an open plan lounge with a modern fitted kitchen and dining area which opens up onto a large rear garden with a paved patio area and large shed to the rear. The first floor comprises of three bedrooms, (two double and one single), along with a modern fitted bathroom and then stairs which lead up to the second floor to the loft room which has an en-suite with modern fitted shower room. The property has off street parking to the front and offers the potential to extend to the rear is subject planning consent.

Honey Lane offers convenience for the commuter providing easy access to the M25 intersection and M11, with bus routes to the neighbouring towns of Epping and Loughton for the Central Line station into London or the br line at Waltham Cross into Liverpool Street or onwards towards Stansted Airport.
